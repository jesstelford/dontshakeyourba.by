How often should I shake my baby?

How hard should I shake my baby?

A: Neither gently nor vigorously. In fact, not at all.

A: No.

I've heard that parents should never shake their babies. Is it OK to have someone else shake my baby?

A: No. No one should ever shake a baby.

I just had a baby yesterday. How long until I can shake my baby?

A: Never. You can never shake your baby.

What is the best technique for shaking my baby?

A: All types of baby-shaking are equally effective and should be avoided.
